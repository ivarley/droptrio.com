Adrian Zavala
(Upright Bass)

An occasional participant with Drop Trio, Adrian brings a different flavor to the mix - great big upright tones, à la Chris Wood, and a penchant for slow developing grooves.

By day, Adrian has been everything from a safe cracker to a rock guitar player. These days he's living the life of an entertainment booker on Houston's north side, and playing with the latin band Ritmo Cha.
