Friday, October 14, 2005 - "Cezanne"
 
Our new disc "Cezanne" is less than a week away from release. But, because I am so nice, and because I pretty much run the show 'round here, I have decided to release one track, "Shelby" on our Live Archive. Cezanne was recorded live and mostly acoustic at Houston's premier jazz venue Cezanne's. New songs on the album include "Luna," "L.U.G." and "Shelby". The album also includes interpretations of two songs from our improvised studio album "Leap", and extended treatments of six songs from our first album, "Big Dipper". Check out the press release for the album and our new press kit.

Please go to our shows page for upcoming CD release shows in your town.
