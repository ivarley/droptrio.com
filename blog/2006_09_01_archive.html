Friday, September 29, 2006 - back on the horse
 
Last night's show at the Elephant Room was the joint. Thanks for coming out, everybody. Thanks also to KUT for having us on the air, we met lots of new folks at the show last night who heard us on the radio.

Now we're on the road up to Tulsa for a show at Tsunami Sushi tonight. What's a little driving when it means you get to have great sushi? We've got some videos of giant robots playing keytars to entertain us.
