Sunday, November 26, 2006 - Can't stop the mojo
 
Big thanks to all our fans who came out to the Santomojo festival. It was a rad day of music.

New finds: Moonshine Still (holy crap!), Lazlo Hollyfeld (Jamie was right! they are teh haxor!)

Old favs: Grass, Groovin' Ground, The Greyhounds, ASHS, Moving Matter, Little Brother Project (watch for their new album), Moses Guest, Catfish Whiskey, Blendt, and the SK-1 Project. All amazing!

Plus the first Houston show for Proteus, an Austin-based prog rock band that brought Ian in as a 2nd keyboard player.

Looking forward to next year!

Friday, November 17, 2006 - 2 more Houston shows this year
 
So, the winter is upon us, and we're hunkering down to work more exclusively on our new record. That means that we don't have a lot of shows coming up this year. :(

But there are 2 more big ones for those of you in Houston: Santomojo (11/25) and Cezanne (12/8).

Santomojo is a huge festival with tons of ridiculous bands. It's the Saturday after Thanksgiving, and if you are a music fan in any way shape or form, you should come to this. We go on at 3:45pm, and there's music till 3am.



Cezanne is our old stomping ground, and the location where we recorded our latest CD (titled, efficiently, Cezanne). We'll be playing 3 sets starting at 9pm.

Hope to see you there!

Thursday, November 09, 2006 - Lo Zephyr in Italia
 
A fan in Italy, Tommaso, wrote this poem to go along with our song Wreck Of The Zephyr.
Dancing on the Zephyr

E’ veramente bello pensare a te come primavera che torna leggera e rapida in un sorriso di una giovane che aspetta la felicità e la verità giovane che si diverte in compagnia di chi ama perché cerca amore giovane che vuole crescere per fuggire da quel presente senza pace realtà buia e povera di Spirito lo sapevi? anime bimbe smarrite nel cielo azzurro non lo sapevamo è veramente bello pensare a te perché so che lo Spirito di verità ci sostiene e la vita che non vedevamo ci accudisce ora come allora madre amorevole che attende risposta dai suoi piccoli e è come se ancora tu ci fossi perché nella tua tu sei nella vita allora corri in questa primavera della memoria e delle anime balla e ridi piena di cuore ora.
E poi sempre.
Quando saremo lontano da questo tempo.

Bonus points to the best English translation!
