Thursday, January 12, 2006 - Weekend Show Details
 
Here's the details for our two TX shows this weekend.

Friday night, January 13th, we're at the Last Concert Cafe in Houston. Grass will go on first at 10pm, and Drop Trio will hit the stage at 11:15. (Double check this spot tomorrow before the show, just to make sure on the times.) Cover is $5.

Last Concert Cafe is at 1403 Nance. To get there, from points west (downtown, Montrose, Heights, etc.), just take I-10 East to the Hardy/McKee/Nance exit. Hang two quick rights at the end of the ramp, and the cafe is ahead on your right. Their mexican food rules, so go early for dinner.

Saturday night, we'll be in Austin at Ruta Maya. Audioflava plays first at 9, then Grass at 10:15, then Drop Trio at 11:30 (again, check here for time updates; this isn't what Ruta Maya has on their site, so we could have it backwards). Cover is $5.

Ruta Maya is at 3601 South Congress - just north of 71 (Ben White), up a hill on the east side of the street (look for the watertower). They've got good munchies too, so bring your appetite.

See you there!
