Sunday, September 26, 2004 - Fat Cat's and Houston Rock
 
Made it to Fat Cat's (aka Mary Jane's) last night to listen to some great music.

Moonshot Radio made their way down from Dallas to open the show. They played a tight set of bopping songs. Arthur Yoria made the scene with his guitar and crooning.

The Astra Heights brothers made a homecoming from Los Angeles and got the crowd out for a rocking set. Too bad the've moved away from Houston - these rockers put on a smart and fun show.

Modultor capped off the night - super tight. These guys are hitting the road pretty hard these days - glad to catch them while they're in town. Awesome set - plenty of far out keys, songs about robots - imagine that, Drop Trio digs these guys.

Sure was nice to have a night of great music as a spectator.
