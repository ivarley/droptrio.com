Tuesday, December 19, 2006 - Drummin' at Antones
 
So, all you Austin folks know about the rad show we're playing on Thursday at Antone's, right? Good.

Well, guess what? Due to a wacky scheduling conflict, it turns out that Nuje will be hundreds of miles away in Baton Rouge at show time. Never fear, though, we've got backup - we'll be having one mister Matt Coleman (from Collect All Five) on the kit.



He's got funk coming out of his pores, so you won't be disappointed. Come on out for a holiday special from outer space.
