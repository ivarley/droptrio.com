Thursday, February 23, 2006 - Benefit for Richard Cholakian, 4/26
 


On December 22, 2005, our friend and fellow musician Richard Cholakian accidentally took a metal wire brush to his eye while on a gig. If you've ever seen Richard play, you know he's enough of a crazy man for something like this to be possible. A portion of his right eye's cornea was ripped off. Ouch!

For the days that follwed, with the support of his close friends, and expert eye doctors at Northwest Medical, his eye recovered fully and came back to working condition. The doctor was amazed at the miraculous recovery.

Happiness soon turned to despair - the medical bills for ambulance fees, hospital fees, and medical specialists were over the top. But with true compassion and brotherly love, fellow musicians Steve Steele and Miltonio DeCoronado spearheaded setting up a benefit concert to help offset these costs.

Anyone who's worked in the music industry in Houston has probably met (or even played with) Richard. He's an awesome guy and needs our support. Come out to Sam's Concert Pub on the evening of Sunday, April 26th, to show your support for this pillar of our community. Drop Trio plays at 10:30.

Wednesday, February 08, 2006 - Podcasts
 
Looking for cool podcasts? Here's a couple we can suggest.

CC 365 - A 365 day calendar of music for your iPod. A tune a day. Drop Trio's Melody-Melody was featured this past Sunday.

Live Music Records - A podcast of live shows at Clark's Recording Studio, a bar in downtown Houston. Drop Trio will be featured on Tuesday, 2/11 doing a live all-improvised set.

The Bugle Boy - Podcast of live shows from our favorite venue in La Grange, TX. Drop Trio's recent show there will be featured on the podcast soon!

RedJazz Podcast - Great jazz tracks from around the internet. A couple drop trio tunes featured in this episode.

Spot One - Our friend Ant Boogie from Collective Hallucination runs this podcast about local music issues. Look for a drop trio drop in sometime soon ...

And of course, don't forget ... Drop Trio's Live Archive Podcast - we haven't put up a new one for a minute, but we will soon ...

If you run podcast, you can legally use Drop Trio's music in your podcast. Check it out, yo.
