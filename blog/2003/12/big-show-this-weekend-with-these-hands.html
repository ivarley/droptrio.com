Thursday, December 11, 2003 -
 
Big show this weekend: With These Hands: A Benefit For Alejandro Escovedo at the Continental Club on Saturday night. There will be some big names there for sure, like Joe Ely, as well as some big local names like John Evans and Hayes Carl. Drop Trio will be crunching some prog funk at around 10pm, and our friends Clouseaux, The Defenestration Unit, and the Waxploitation DJs are on the bill as well. See our showdates page for more info; here's the flyer:



As for other stuff, there's no dearth of music this weekend in Houston! Here are some shows by friends of ours:

Thursday

  • Dana Rogers @ River Cafe - Great Jazz singer with killer band (Ken Ward & more) ... it's her birthday, go show her some love!
  • The Buddhacrush @ Sherlock's (West Gray) - Jazzy funk pop rock
  • The Psychodillos & Groovin Ground @ Rhythm Room - Two awesome jam bands
  • James Garcia @ Vintage Bar - Great original folk blues tunes
  • 14er @ Mary Jane's Fat Cat - Virtuosic prog punk
Friday

  • Tru Sol @ SRO Sports Bar (Northwest Mall) - Smooth mellow funky soul rock music, and that boy can sing
  • Mandy Smith Group @ 12 Spot - Mandy's Holiday Party, make sure to go by!
  • Hilary Sloan @ Mary Jane's Fat Cat - The best Honky Tonk country in H-town
  • Clouseaux @ Rhythm Room - Eclectic mystery tiki music? Yes, and also voted best new band in Houston, 2003 HPMA.
  • Brian Kalinec @ Market Square Bar & Grill - Finely crafted folk songs
  • Mekka @ Woodlake Pub - Stronger, Louder, and Packed Full of Energy. She kicks butt.
  • Shannon Cutts, Brent Adair & Jim Kam (Chapman stick player) @ Harmony Coffee House - Shannon and Brent are great, and they'll be trading songs on stage. And if you've never seen a Chapman stick player, go check it out, it's amazing to watch.
Saturday

  • Alejandro Escovedo Benefit @ Continental Club - The main event, of course. :)
  • (1pm show) Wayside Drive @ St. Arnold's Brewery - Up and coming Houston jam band - word on the street is, they'll have a real M3 organ on stage with 'em, being played by Mr. Alan Cunningham.
  • Blendt & Vibe Committee @ Last Concert Cafe
  • Brian Kalinec, Lisa Novak & Melinda Mones @ The Harp
  • Us & Them @ Pennison's #2 (Copperfield)
  • Lights In The Heights
Sunday

  • Thomas Helton & Friends @ Super Happy Fun Land - Playing some of Thomas' original compositions! This will be a truly cool show.
  • Bruce James @ Rhythm Room - Old school soul sounds, original tunes by Bruce, with the legendary Teri Greene on guitar and the Moses Guest rhythm section backing 'em up.
  • Richard Cholakian's Jam @ Dean's Credit Clothing - The jam that keeps growing! Go see for yourself.
  • Jane Vandiver Singers @ Ovations - One of Houston's finest jazz singers and her crew.
  • Harry Sheppard @ River Cafe - Vibraphone fireworks by the Free Radicals' front man.
  • Max G. Arnold Xmas Party @ The Proletariat

Get out there and see some live music!
