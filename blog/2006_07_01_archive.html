Tuesday, July 18, 2006 - New Calendar Tools
 
We've added a few new ways you can keep track of the Drop Trio shows calendar.

First, if you use Google Calendar, you can now subscribe to Drop Trio's public shows. That way, our shows calendar will appear as an additional calendar when you log in to google calendar.

If you use other calendar software like Apple's iCal or Mozilla Sunbird, you can subscribe to our ical feed. Then our events will show up in your calendar app.

Or if you don't do either of those, but want to see the latest up-to-the-minute changes and additions in our schedule, you can view a live calendar on the web.

Who loves ya? That's right, Google does. Oh, and us.

Monday, July 10, 2006 - Shows & Voting
 
Howdy all! Hope you're having a good summer. We've been taking it real chill-like for a few weeks, but we're about ready to pop back into gear again.

First off, please consider voting for Drop Trio in the "Best Jazz" category on the Houston Press Music Awards.

Second, come see us this weekend in Houston! We've got 2 cool (and unusual) shows coming up.

On Friday, we'll be at Dean's Credit Clothing celebrating the 11 year anniversary of the Technology Bytes radio show on KPFT. It's open to the public and free to get in (21 + over only), so come unleash your inner geek. Or hell, even your outer geek.

Then, on Saturday, we'll be performing at Super Happy Fun Land as part of JamHouston's Extemporama series! Should be a wacky night. We'll be sharing the stage with 3 of our favorite weird-ass bands in Houston: Muzak John, Friendship Bracelet, and Blabat (with Jarle Lillemoen from Grass and Groovin' Ground on guitar!).

See you then!
