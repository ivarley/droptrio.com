Monday, December 13, 2004 - We're done
 
Brasil, The Vortex (Beaumont) and the Proletariat rocked. The new songs are coming together nicely. In fact last night at the Proletariat we actually played an entire set of "new" songs. By "new" I mean, nothing from our first CD. This was a first. For those of you keeping score, here was the set list:

Big S.O.,
Mothership
Shelby Gets a Haircut
Barmecide
Robot Suit I
-
Cognac Morning
L.U.G

We'd like to say thank you to everyone for making '04 a great year for Drop Trio. Our next show will be at Helios January 29th. We'll have more details as the date approaches.

The live archive will continue to be updated weekly during our break as well as the blog. Funk Jazz Fridays will continue at Brasil in '05 as well.

Don't forget to take some time and vert with your family over the holidays.

Friday, December 10, 2004 - Three More
 
This is it folks! The final Drop shows of '04 are here. After this weekend we are actually taking over a month off to finish writing some new material. We will be back in the studio in March '05 so we can get another album out by the fall.

Tonight Brasil. Funk Jazz Fridays. New songs.

Saturday Beaumont. We return to the Vortex. KVLU will be playing tracks from "Leap" tonight sometime after 9pm. You can listen online here. Also check out the new addition to the live archive. It is a selection from our first Beaumont show from earlier this year.

Sunday we're back in Houston at the Proletariat. This will be a rockin' show so don't miss out. We go on just before 10. Also on the bill Southern Backtones and AM60.

Oh yeah, Rush was nominated for a Grammy. Rule!
