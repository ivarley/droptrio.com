Thursday, January 18, 2007 - Starrfire Session
 
This weekend, we did our third session for the new album, this time at Starrfire recording studio in League City, TX - about half an hour south of Houston. Allen and Chris Portman (from the band More Than Machines ) run the studio, and did an incredible job capturing our live improv.



As usual, we recorded hours of improvisations to be used as source material for the new album. Woot. Thanks for having us, guys.



Then we went home and played Wii. Double woot. More photos here.
