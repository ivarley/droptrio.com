Tuesday, December 06, 2005 - Anybody out there?
 
So, playing spaceship jazz is cool and all, but wouldn't it be rad to actually FIND some spaceships? We thought so too, so we started a SETI @ Home team for Drop Trio. Join up!

(In case you're not familiar, SETI @ Home uses idle time on computers around the world to analyze data from radio telescopes in the hope of finding some intelligent life out there in the universe.)

And yes, this qualifies as the geekiest thing we've done since ... um, like, yesterday.
