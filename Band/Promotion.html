I've asked around a few printing places in my search for cheap printing. I've found that office depot is the cheapest. I can't recall the prices right this second, but once you've figured out what you're going to do for postcard (front or front/back, color, paper), then I can get price quotes for you.
I've found the easiest way to do postcards and not have to worry about things getting cut off is make the postcard 5.5 by 4.25 inches (or 4.25 x 5.5, depending on if it's landscape or portrait, but leave .25 inches around each side of the postcard as a "bleed" area so that the cards can be cut without messing up any words. if you have quark or indesign, you can pull a guide ruler to mark off the .25 inch. Let me know if that helps. Basically you layout of the 4 postcards on a 8.5 x 11 inch document, but assume that a quarter inch on each side could possibly be cut.
