Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are y'all from Houston?

A: Mostly. Patrick lives in Houston. Nuje lives in The Woodlands (a suburb of Houston). Ian used to live in Houston, but he and his wife rent a house in Austin now and he spends most of his time in the van.

Q: You know you guys really need a singer?

A: No, in fact, we don't. One of us is a fantastic singer. However, Drop Trio plays instrumental music.

Q: You know you guys really need a guitar player?

A: Although we enjoy having all sorts of folks sit in with us from time to time, Drop Trio just isn't geared for full time guitarist.

Q: You know I should join your band and play guitar?

A: Sounds great! Um, have your people call our people. We'll totally get right back to you.

Q: Do you know how to play "All of me", "Start Me Up", "Brickhouse", or "American Idiot?"

A: Yes -- but we won't - unless you pay us lots of money. Like, enough to never have to play said song again.

Q: Where do y'all play?

A: Lots of places - mostly in Texas, Louisiana, and Oklahoma. We've got the whole list at droptrio.com.

Q: Would like to be the backing band for my next studio album?

A: Absolutely. We've done it several times, love it, and are pretty good at it - whatever the project.

Q: Would you compose some music for my film project, TV show, or infomercial?

A: Absolutely. You can license our existing music from magnatune.com - those guys rock. If have something else in mind, let us know.

Q: Are you called "The Drop Trio" or just "Drop Trio"?

A: Drop Trio. DROP TRIO! There's no "the"!! NO THE!! If you put a "the" on the front of our band name, we reserve the right to call you "THE big moron who keeps saying 'the' Drop Trio".

Q: I've heard a lot about you, but I've never seen you play. What's the music like?

A: Spaceship jazz.
