Here is some venue information:
  • Moe's Alley. - http://www.moesalley.com/
  • Kuumbwa Jazz Center - http://www.kuumbwajazz.org/contact/index.htm
  • The Attic - http://www.theatticsantacruz.com/booking.htm
I think Moe's Alley might be a good fit. Looking at their page I don't see any contact information for a booking agent. They have a number to call for renting the club out. The folks at that number might be able to direct you to their booking agent.

The Kuumbwa webpage is similar, I don't see booking agent contact information. They are really nice folks and I suspect they might be the most helpful in directing you towards a booking agent.

"The Attic" is a restaurant and cafe that also has musical acts.

There are some other venues in Santa Cruz that I don't frequent as much:
  • The Catalyst - http://www.catalystclub.com/
  • The Coastline Brewery (a new pub that has live music) - http://www.coastlinebrewery.com/
The Catalyst frequently books well known acts (I've seen John Mclaughlin, Parliament and others there). The Coastline might be a bit small for you guys.
