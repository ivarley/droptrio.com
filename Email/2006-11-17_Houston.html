We've got 2 more great shows in Houston before the end of the year:
Santomojo Festival, 11/25
Houston has long lacked a worthy music festival of it's own, but the time has come. Introducuing:
Santomojo Music & Arts Summit
At the Meridian (1503 Chartres)
Saturday, 11/25 (see the schedule)
Tickets are $20 in advance, all ages

The list of bands is unbelieveable - Mike Dillon, Spoonfed Tribe, Quodia (w/ members of King Crimson) ... not to mention a lot of DT friends like Moses Guest, LBP, Plump, Blendt, ASHS, Grass, etc). There will be food, art, & more. Drop Trio plays at 3:45 on the main stage.

Cezanne, 12/8
Many of you have asked us why we don't play at Cezanne more, especially since recording our 3rd album there. Well, here it is!
Drop Trio @ Cezanne
4100 Montrose (upstairs from the Black Lab)
Friday, 12/8 @ 9pm
3 sets, $10, 21+ only

Hope to see you there! Check out the blog for more regular info.
