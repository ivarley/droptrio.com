JamHouston.com
Have you heard of JamHouston.com? It's a new site devoted to jam, improvised & experimental music in Houston. Started by Nino Batista, it features:
  • Streaming radio with a killer playlist
  • Active forums (we're on there)
  • Reviews, news, a blog, compilation CDs, etc.

Check out a great writeup about JamHouston in today's Houston Calling. And make sure you come to the kickoff party on April 24th at the Meridian (before the Medeski Martin & Wood show)! Email us for more details.
