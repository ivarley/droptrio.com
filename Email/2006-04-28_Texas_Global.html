ATX Fest in Austin This Weekend

This weekend marks the first year of the new ATX fest in Austin! There are a zillion great bands on the bill - Tea Leaf Green, Olospo, Spoonfed Tribe, Moses Guest, Gnappy, Collect All Five, Groovin' Ground, LBP, and many more.

Come to ATX fest!

Drop Trio plays Saturday at 5pm. There are 1- and 2-day passes available at the door, so don't miss this great lineup!

By the way, to our San Antonio friends, we are NOT playing at Sunset Station tonight. We'll be back in June - until then, take the opportunity to hit the first night of ATX fest instead!
