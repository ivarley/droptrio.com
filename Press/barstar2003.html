Vote for the Best of Houston on BarStar!

Here's a ballot for the BarStar Best of 2003 Readers Choice Awards. Deadline for returning ballots is Friday, Jan 9.

Rules

  1. One entry per person per email.
  2. For nominees with common names or more than one location, please be specific (i.e. Bob's Beergarten on S. State Street).
  3. Ballots containing identical email addresses or too many suspiciously identical answers will be disqualified. So no ballot stuffing.

To vote, copy and paste the text below and email it (with your answers filled in of course) to postmaster@barstar.com.

Drop Trio's endorsements are (naturally) Drop Trio for all band categories, Nino Batista on Bass, Mike Blattel on Drums, Ian Varley on Keys, and Big Dipper for best album. Then add in your other Houston favorites!
