To subscribe to the Drop Trio Live Archive, just follow these steps.

  1. Download iTunes version 4.9 or later. [Get it here]
  2. Copy this URL: http://feeds.feedburner.com/DropTrioLiveArchive
  3. Go to iTunes, and click on the "Advanced" menu, then select "Subscribe To Podcast".
  4. Paste in the URL you copied in step 2. Click "OK".
That's it! You're now subscribed to the Drop Trio Podcast. Enjoy!
